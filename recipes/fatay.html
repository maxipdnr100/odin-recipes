Odin Recipes: Fatay

In order to make the best fatay , you will need the following ingredients for the recipe.

Ingredients for the dough:

  • 1kg of flour
  • 20g salt
  • 500-600ml of water (room temperature)
  • 10g dry yeast
  • 2tbsp olive oil

Ingredients for the filling:

  • 2 big onions
  • 1 big red bell pepper
  • 1 big tomato
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 1kg minced beef meet
  • 1 tbsp Baharat spices (cumin, clove, nutmeg, coriander, cardamom, cinnamon, pepper)
  • juice of 4 lemons
  • salt and pepper to taste

Once you have everything for the recipe, now, you must follow the next steps.

Instructions for the dough:

  1. Dissolve the yeast in the lukewarm water, add the olive oil, and mix.
  2. Place the flour in a large bowl, add the salt and mix so that the yeast does not get in direct touch with the salt
  3. Pour in the water mixture and knead until the dough is smooth and elastic
  4. Transfer it to a lightly floured surface, knead a bit more, and cover
  5. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes, or until it triples in size

Instructions for the filling and making:

  1. Meanwhile, finely dice the onions, bell peppers, and tomato. Mince the garlic
  2. Combine the vegetables with the minced beef, tomato paste, mint, parsley, baharat spices, cinnamon, salt, and pepper
  3. Add the lemon juice and mix thoroughly
  4. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours
  5. Preheat the oven to very high heat (about 250°C / 480°F)
  6. Divide the dough into balls of about 60 grams
  7. Roll each ball into a thin circle of about 20 cm diameter
  8. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle. Close into a triangle shape, leaving a small opening in the middle
  9. Place on a baking tray with parchment paper and bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the dough is cooked and lightly golden
  10. Serve hot, alone or with a light yogurt dip

Now, you have the best fatay of your city in your hands!
