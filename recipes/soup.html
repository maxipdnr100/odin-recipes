Odin Recipes: Soup

In order to make the best soup, you will need the following ingredients for the recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 200g chopped vegetables such as onions, celery and carrots
  • 300g potatoes cubed
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 700ml stock
  • crème fraîche and fresh herbs, to serve

Once you have everything for the recipe, now, you must follow the next steps.

Instructions:

  1. Fry the vegetables and potatoes in a pan with the oil for a few minutes until beginning to soften
  2. Cover with the stock and simmer for 10-15 mins until the veg is tender. Blend until smooth, then season. Serve with a dollop of crème fraîche and some fresh herbs. Will freeze for up to one month

Now, you have the best soup of your city in your hands!
